# ADR 0005: Secondary projects and private work

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Several useful projects should be represented without competing with the primary featured projects. There is also a local work tool that may be valuable later but requires heavy sanitization.

## Decision

Group GodForge, YapHub, Atlas, and the future sanitized internal tool under "Also Built" or similar secondary sections.

### GodForge

Frame as a Smite 2 randomizer/community tool for Discord communities, including god and item generation for casual play and randomized league formats.

### YapHub

Frame as a Discord utility for temporary voice channels and cleaner community voice workflows.

### Atlas

Frame as Labs/Experimental: an early local LLM, RAG, and homelab automation experiment. Avoid overclaiming future "Jarvis" behavior before it is demonstrably mature.

### Sanitized internal tool

Do not publish until heavily sanitized. It may eventually become a private-work case study with:

- No employer/company name
- No proprietary data
- No real screenshots unless rebuilt with fake data
- No sensitive implementation details
- Generalized problem and solution framing
- A confidentiality note

## Consequences

- Secondary work can prove breadth and real usage without cluttering the main project hierarchy.
- Private professional work can be included later without confidentiality risk.
