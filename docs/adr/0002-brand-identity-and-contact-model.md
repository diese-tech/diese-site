# ADR 0002: Brand identity and contact model

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The owner uses "Diese" as a common online identity and username, while the portfolio also needs to be discoverable and professional for recruiters, LinkedIn, and employers searching by name.

## Decision

Use "Diese" as the site brand and visual identity, while keeping the professional name visible and searchable.

- Brand/nav identity: `Diese`
- Professional name: `Dustin Nieves`
- Optional informal reference: Dustin "Diese" Nieves may appear in about/footer copy, but should not be the primary professional label everywhere.
- SEO/title identity should include Dustin Nieves.
- GitHub: https://github.com/diese-tech
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/dustin-nieves/
- Email: nievesdustinl@yahoo.com
- Resume link: `/resume.pdf` placeholder until the PDF is added.

Do not publish the phone number directly on the public homepage. Keep phone contact in the resume PDF only to reduce scraping/spam risk.

## Consequences

- The site can feel branded and personal without being anonymous to employers.
- Recruiters can match the portfolio to LinkedIn and resume materials.
- Contact UX should prioritize email, LinkedIn, GitHub, and resume download.
